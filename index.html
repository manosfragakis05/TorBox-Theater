⚠️ Crucial: Do not just download the AdGuard App. Apple disables app-blockers inside PWAs. You must follow the Manual Profile steps below.

Get the Profile

2

Approve Download

When prompted by iOS, tap "Allow" to download the configuration file. You will see a "Profile Downloaded" message.

3

Activate Profile

1. Open iPhone Settings
2. Tap Profile Downloaded (near the top)
3. Tap Install (top right) and enter passcode
4. Tap Install again to confirm
